## Unplanned: universal-capture n8n found crash-looping (new discovery)
While auditing n8n-zuij, found a *second*, unrelated, completely
untracked container also named `n8n` (project `universal-capture`,
living in `mancinicn`'s home dir, no git history, not in
`HANDOVER.md`'s services table) — continuously crash-looping since
its creation on 2026-07-13T01:02 (2500+ restarts by the time this was
found, ~2 days of silent failure, same "broke and nobody noticed"
pattern as the NAS n8n-postgres incident behind ADR-014).
- **Root cause**: UID mismatch. Its compose pins `user: 1000:1000` but
  the bind-mounted host data dir (`/home/mancinicn/universal-capture/
  data/n8n/`) is owned by `mancinicn`, uid 1001 — `EACCES` reading
  `.n8n/config`, forever.
- Its Traefik labels claim `n8n.christianmancini.de` — the same
  hostname Christian wants the canonical NAS n8n to eventually serve.
  Confirmed via `curl -H "Host: n8n.christianmancini.de"` against
  Traefik locally: **404**, no valid route at all (Traefik's Docker
  provider never registered it — it's never once reached a running
  state).
- Also on `n8n:latest` (no pin) and has DB password +
  `N8N_ENCRYPTION_KEY` hardcoded in plaintext directly in its
  `docker-compose.yml` (not an env_file), plus its own
  `credentials-export.json`/`credentials.sql` in the data dir.
- **Action taken (explicit go-ahead)**: `docker stop n8n` — killed the
  crash-loop/log spam, all data/volumes left untouched, fully
  reversible. Left the compose project and its data otherwise
  untouched pending Christian's decision on what `universal-capture`
  even is / whether it should exist.

## New roadmap item recorded (not built): public route for canonical n8n
Christian wants `n8n.christianmancini.de` to eventually serve the
canonical NAS n8n, not either of the legacy containers above. Scoped
via two decisions with Christian:

  on the roadmap (`docs/agent-implementation-plan.md`) for its own
  plan, same pattern as the auto-updater and Immich before they were
  built.
